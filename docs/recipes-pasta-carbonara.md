# Simple Pasta Carbonara

A classic Roman pasta dish that's surprisingly easy to make.

## Details
- **Difficulty:** ⭐ Easy
- **Time:** ⏱️ 20 minutes
- **Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

- 400g spaghetti
- 200g guanciale (or pancetta), diced
- 4 large eggs
- 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
- Black pepper to taste
- Salt

## Instructions

1. **Cook pasta** - Bring salted water to boil and add spaghetti. Cook until al dente (about 10 minutes).

2. **Prepare guanciale** - While pasta cooks, fry the guanciale in a large pan until crispy (about 5 minutes).

3. **Mix egg sauce** - In a bowl, whisk together eggs, cheese, and plenty of black pepper. Set aside.

4. **Combine** - Reserve 1 cup pasta water. Drain pasta and add to the pan with guanciale (off heat). Let cool for 30 seconds.

5. **Add sauce** - Pour egg mixture over pasta, tossing constantly. Add pasta water as needed for creamy consistency.

6. **Serve** - Plate immediately and top with extra cheese and pepper.

## 💡 Tips & Variations

- **No guanciale?** Use pancetta or bacon as substitute
- **Keep it creamy** - The key is combining hot pasta with egg sauce OFF the heat
- **Vegetarian version** - Use butter and mushrooms instead of guanciale

# Chocolate Chip Cookies

Classic homemade chocolate chip cookies that are soft and chewy.

## Details
- **Difficulty:** ⭐ Easy
- **Time:** ⏱️ 30 minutes
- **Yield:** 24 cookies

## Ingredients

- 220g butter, softened
- 200g brown sugar
- 100g white sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 280g all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 340g chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. **Preheat** - Heat oven to 375°F (190°C).

2. **Cream butter and sugars** - Beat for 2-3 minutes until light and fluffy.

3. **Add eggs** - Add eggs one at a time, then vanilla. Beat well.

4. **Mix dry ingredients** - In separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.

5. **Combine** - Gradually blend dry mixture into creamed mixture.

6. **Add chocolate** - Stir in chocolate chips.

7. **Bake** - Drop rounded tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake 8-10 minutes until golden brown.

8. **Cool** - Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes, then transfer to wire racks.

## 💡 Tips & Variations

- **Chewier cookies** - Slightly underbake them
- **Add texture** - Mix in nuts, macadamia, or sea salt
- **Brown butter** - Brown the butter first for deeper flavor
